Welcome to the SLUGS documentation!

SLUGS (SeLecive User Grouping System) is a webapp that BSSL will use to manage employees, shows, and general managerial tasks. The goal of this custom built software is to make all of our jobs easier.

If there is anything you think should be a part of slugs, let us know! You can either reach out to the Sysadmin directly (currently Joe Babbitt) or email management.

Overview:

SLUGS is a closed system accessible anywhere for registered employees at slugs.bssl.binghamtonsa.org. It acts as a hubs for employees to register for shows, view details once they've been assigned, and help us save paperwork.
